Let's Keep Moving

Coo Rides is India’s first multi-modal micro mobility service with hybrid docking technology that allows users to rent bicycles and electric bicycles on-demand from designated stations across a city. Our mobility networks allow for a sustainable transportation system that is fun, simple to use and is kind to the environment. We are committed to bringing our systems to a city near you and keep you moving!
Coo Rides is the first and only micromobility operator from India to have been accredited by the UK government's mobility procurement accreditation agency, Co-Mobility UK (CoMoUK)

How it works

Download

Download the COO Rides app from the Playstore or App store

Scan

Tap Scan to Ride and scan the QR code on the cycle.

Unlock

Verify the details and the pricing. If all seems good, then just Unlock the Bike and Yay! You are good to go!
