About Coo Rides Bike Share:

Coo Rides is India’s first multi-modal micro mobility service with hybrid docking technology that allow users to rent vehicles (bikes, e-bikes, e-scooters and e-rickshaws) on demand from designated stations across a city. Unlike other bikeshare networks, the hybrid docking technology enables users of the system to locate their preferred type of vehicle at designated stations without the hassle, pause their ride temporarily in between trips and lock vehicles at their destination stations all at the touch of a button on their smartphone.

Our mobility networks allow for a sustainable transportation system that is fun, simple to use and is kind to the environment. We are committed to bringing our systems to a city near you and keep you moving! Join us now!

About ParXsys Mobility Systems Pvt Ltd:

ParXsys Mobility Systems Pvt Ltd a Bengaluru based micro-mobility technology company focussed towards implementing electric bicycle sharing services across India since 2016. The company has designed and developed the world’s first hybrid bicycle share patent pending technology that enables it to operate a fleet of electric bicycles that can switch between dock enabled and dockless operation seamlessly based on environment and on-demand user preference. It is promoted by the founders of Kerberon Automations Pvt. Ltd. – responsible for setting up of India’s first automated bicycle share for Bengaluru Metro Rail Corporation Limited in 2012. The company owns and operates the micro-mobility brand called Coo Rides that offers bicycle and electric bicycles to registered users via the Coo Rides App. The service is available in Thane (Maharashtra), Naya Raipur (Chhattisgarh) and Davanagere (Karnataka) and soon poised to start operations in Mumbai and Bengaluru. The company has dedicated inhouse technology teams based out of Bengaluru for software, hardware and mechanical engineering for a complete inhouse IP technology ecosystem. Backed by India's leading OOH company - Signpost India, as our primary investors, ParXsys has an unmatched capability of operating large scale public operations with ease.

The specially designed anti-rust and lightweight aluminium electric bicycles are available to users from special parking racks placed across the urban geography of a city. Users can unlock and turn on the bicycle remotely from their apps after locating them on a in-app map and use them for their last and first mile commutes. The electric bicycles are designed to operate at a maximum speed of 25KMPH enabling users to navigate in urban traffic by keeping up with the speed of conventional vehicle, making it safe to use in traffic as well. The hybrid dock technology enables users to lock the bicycle even in locations where parking racks are not available in between their rides.
